GDPR Compliance and Your Rights

MegaV VPN fully complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union and European Economic Area.

1. Your Rights Under GDPR:

Right of Access (Article 15):
• Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
• Understand how and why your data is processed
• Receive information within 30 days

Right to Rectification (Article 16):
• Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
• Update your account information at any time
• Request corrections to technical data

Right to Erasure (Article 17 - "Right to be Forgotten"):
• Request deletion of your personal data
• Delete your account and all associated data
• Data will be permanently removed within 30 days

Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18):
• Limit how we use your personal data
• Suspend data processing while investigating concerns
• Maintain data without active processing

Right to Data Portability (Article 20):
• Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
• Transfer your data to another service provider
• Export account settings and preferences

Right to Object (Article 21):
• Object to processing based on legitimate interests
• Opt-out of optional data collection
• Withdraw consent for analytics and crash reporting

2. Legal Basis for Processing:

• Contract Performance: Providing VPN services you requested
• Legitimate Interests: Service improvement and security
• Consent: Optional features (analytics, crash reporting)
• Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws

3. Data Minimization:

We collect only:
• Data necessary for VPN service functionality
• Information required for account management
• Technical data for service improvement (with consent)

We do NOT collect:
• Browsing history or online activity
• Personal files or device content
• Location data beyond server selection
• Biometric or sensitive personal information

4. How to Exercise Your Rights:

Contact our Data Protection Officer:
• Email: support@megav.store
• Subject: "GDPR Request - [Your Right]"
• Include: Your account email and request details

Response Time:
• We respond within 30 days as required by GDPR
• Complex requests may take up to 60 days (we'll notify you)
• No fees for standard requests

5. Supervisory Authority:

If you believe we haven't addressed your concerns:
• You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority
• EU residents can contact their national supervisory authority
• We will cooperate fully with any investigation

6. International Data Transfers:

• Data stored in privacy-friendly jurisdictions
• Adequate safeguards in place for international transfers
• No data stored in countries with mass surveillance laws
• Standard Contractual Clauses used where applicable

7. Children's Privacy:

• MegaV VPN is not intended for users under 16
• We do not knowingly collect data from children
• If you believe we have collected child data, contact us immediately

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring full GDPR compliance.
